COSMOS2015 MAGPHYS+AGN catalog

Here are the SDSS+WISE MAGPHYS catalogs in Chang et al. (2015):


SDSS+WISE MAGPHYS input catalog: [MRT] file; and the [readme] file.


SDSS+WISE MAGPHYS output catalog: [MRT] file; and the [readme] file.


They contain all 858,365 galaxies from the NYU-VAGC spectroscopic galaxy sample with good redshift measurements. We recommend to use the MAGPHYS modeling results for objects with FLAG=1 (633,205 out of 858,365). These are all z < 0.2 galaxies with reliable aperture corrections based on size measurements from Simard et al. (2011), good WISE photometry (FLAG_W?=1 or 2), and good-quality SED fits (FLAG_CHI2=1).
